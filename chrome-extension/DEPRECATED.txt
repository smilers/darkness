Darkness - Deprecation Notice
==============================

Darkness has been deprecated as of July 2025 due to Google's deprecation
of Chrome Manifest V2 extensions. The extension is no longer functional.

Updates are published for existing installed users to align the extension
with its deprecated status.

Changes made:
- Updated the extension popup to display a deprecation notice
- Removed references to external services no longer in use
- No new functionality has been added
